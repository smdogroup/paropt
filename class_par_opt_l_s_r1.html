Detailed Description

This class implements a limited-memory SR1 updating scheme based on computed differences in the step and Lagrange graidents during a line search.

This is based on the paper by Byrd, Nocedal and Schnabel, "Representations of quasi-Newton matrices and their use in limited-memory methods".

The limited-memory SR1 formula takes the following form:

b0*I - Z*M^{-1}*Z^{T}

Here b0 is a scalar, M is a matrix and Z is a rectagular matrix stored as a series of vectors.
